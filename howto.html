How to install skins:

  1. Open this website in Safari on your iPhone
  2. Find a skin you want, tap it, and tap "Download now"
  3. When the download completes, open the Files app and navigate to the downloaded skin
  4. Tap the share icon and choose "Copy to Delta"
  5. Go to Delta settings and choose the controller skin you just downloaded
  6. Enjoy :)
